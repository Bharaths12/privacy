Privacy Policy

This Chrome extension is designed to help users highlight and match keywords (such as GenAI sitelink headers and related terms) on web pages they visit.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information, financial information, or browsing history.

The extension only processes the text content of the current web page locally on your device to perform highlighting and keyword matching. No data is sent to external servers.

Permissions

- activeTab: Required to analyze the content of the currently open web page when the extension is active.
- scripting: Required to inject highlighting scripts into the page for keyword detection.
- storage: Required to save your custom keyword list and highlight preferences (such as colors).

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use, sell, or share data with third-party services. Synonym matching and fuzzy matching logic are handled locally or through free public APIs (if enabled by the user), without storing personal data.
